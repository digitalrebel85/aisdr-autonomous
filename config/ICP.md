# ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) - Marketing Agencies

## Target: Marketing Agencies Serving B2B SaaS

### Firmographics
- **Size:** 10-50 employees
- **Revenue:** £500K - £5M annually
- **Location:** UK primarily, US secondary
- **Services:** Demand gen, lead gen, content marketing, paid ads

### Pain Points
1. **SDR turnover is brutal** - Training, churn, replacement costs
2. **Can't scale outreach** - Human SDRs hit capacity at ~50/day
3. **Clients want more leads** - Pressure to deliver volume + quality
4. **Don't have AI expertise** - Want AI results without hiring AI team

### Buying Triggers
- Just lost a big client (need to win new ones)
- Hired new account director (ambitious, wants quick wins)
- Raised funding/expanding (scaling pain)
- Seasonal slow period (Q1, summer) - desperate for pipeline

### Decision Makers
1. **Founder/MD** (agencies <20 people)
2. **Head of Growth/New Business Director** (agencies 20-50)
3. **Client Services Director** (concerned about delivery)

### Where They Hang Out
- LinkedIn (primary)
- Agency Slack communities (e.g., Agency Collective)
- Events: BrightonSEO, Turing Fest, SaaStock
- Podcasts: The Agency Hour, Agency Trailblazer

### Keywords in Profiles
- "B2B SaaS marketing"
- "Demand generation"
- "Account-based marketing"
- "Marketing automation"
- "Lead generation"

### Red Flags (Don't Target)
- Agencies focused on e-commerce/DTC
- Brand-only agencies (no performance marketing)
- <5 employees (can't afford £1k/mo)
- >100 employees (have internal teams already)

## Campaign Focus

### Angle 1: White-Label AI SDR
"Become an AI-powered agency overnight. We become your SDR department."

### Angle 2: Overflow Capacity  
"Your team at max? We handle the overflow outreach."

### Angle 3: New Service Line
"Launch 'AI-powered outreach' as a premium service to your clients."

## Messaging Themes
- "Replace 3 SDRs with 1 AI that never sleeps"
- "Your clients want AI outreach. Now you can deliver it."
- "Scale without the hiring headache"
